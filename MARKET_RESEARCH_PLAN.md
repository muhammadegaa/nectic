# Market Research Plan: Using Public Data

**Approach:** Use existing market research, competitor analysis, and industry reports instead of primary interviews.

---

## RESEARCH SOURCES

### 1. Industry Reports & Studies
- Gartner reports on automation/AI adoption
- McKinsey reports on operational efficiency
- Deloitte reports on digital transformation
- Industry-specific reports (healthcare, finance, retail)

### 2. Competitor Analysis
- What questions do competitors ask?
- What pain points do they target?
- What features do they prioritize?
- What's their assessment flow?

### 3. Public Forums & Communities
- Reddit (r/smallbusiness, r/entrepreneur, r/operations)
- Hacker News discussions
- LinkedIn groups
- Industry forums

### 4. Product Reviews & Case Studies
- G2 reviews (what do customers complain about?)
- Capterra reviews (what features are requested?)
- Case studies from competitors
- Success stories

### 5. Market Data
- Market size reports
- Pricing benchmarks
- Adoption rates
- ROI studies

---

## KEY RESEARCH QUESTIONS

### Pain Points (What to Research)
1. What are the top 3 operational inefficiencies for mid-market companies?
2. What manual processes waste the most time?
3. What automation opportunities have highest ROI?
4. What are the biggest frustrations with current tools?
5. What prevents companies from adopting automation?

### Market Validation (What to Research)
1. What's the market size for automation tools?
2. What's the average budget for automation solutions?
3. What's the typical sales cycle?
4. Who makes the buying decision?
5. What's the willingness to pay?

### Competitive Landscape (What to Research)
1. Who are the main competitors?
2. What do they do well?
3. What do they do poorly?
4. What gaps exist in the market?
5. What's their pricing?

---

## RESEARCH OUTPUTS

### 1. Validated Pain Points (Top 3-5)
Based on research, identify:
- Most common pain points
- Highest ROI opportunities

### 2. Simplified Assessment (5-7 Questions)
Based on research:
- Primary pain point (from validated list)
- Company size
- Budget (from market data)
- Timeline (from market data)
- Decision maker (from market data)

### 3. Feature Priorities
Based on research:
- What features matter most?
- What features can we skip?
- What's the MVP feature set?
